About Us

16+ Years of Excellence in Construction and Valuation Services

Our Story

Sri Kumaran Construction started in the year 2008, with over 16 years of experience in the construction field. We have exhibited high quality of construction, built with the government and private projects such as:

  • Residential Buildings
  • School Buildings
  • Marriage Halls
  • Commercial Buildings

Our projects are built to provide exceptional life experiences, security and value. We have expertise in land acquisition, understanding the customer needs, with the execution capabilities.

We have a skilled expertised team of 250 members, with technically qualified supervisors. Moreover, we have achieved Rs 5 crore/annum turnover of the financial transaction.

With innovation, quality, and sustainability at the core of our work and a unique customer centric approach has put us on the league of the best and most trusted.
